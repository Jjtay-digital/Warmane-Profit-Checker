Selling Tips

1. The "Raid Reset" Gold Mine (Wednesday/Thursday)

This is the most important rule for a Jewelcrafter.

The Logic: This is when raid lockouts reset. Players go into Naxxramas, get new gear drops, and immediately need to socket them with gems.

The Strategy: Have 20–50 of the most popular cuts (Runed Scarlet Ruby, Solid Sky Sapphire, etc.) ready to post on Wednesday evening.

Pricing: You can usually list gems for 10–20% higher than the weekend price, and they will still sell instantly.

2. The "Pre-Raid" Rush (Daily 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM)

This is the peak time for Engineers selling consumables.

The Logic: Raiders are logging in, checking their bags, and realizing they are out of Global Thermal Sapper Charges or Mana Injectors.

The Strategy: Post your Sappers, Bombs, and Repair Bots (Scrapbots) in stacks of 10 or 20 during this window.

The Tip: Avoid posting these at 2:00 AM; the "undercutters" will bury your listing by the time the evening raiders log on.

3. The "Sunday Night" Material Dump

The Logic: Weekend "casual" players have spent Saturday and Sunday farming Saronite and Titanium. By Sunday night, the Auction House is flooded with raw materials, causing prices to bottom out.

The Strategy:

  • Buy your raw Saronite Ore and Eternals on Sunday night when they are cheapest.
  • Sell your finished jewelry or cut gems on Tuesday/Wednesday.
  • Never sell raw mats on a Sunday; you are competing with too many farmers.

4. The "Daily Quest" Window (JC Specific)

The Logic: Every day, the Jewelcrafting daily quest requires a specific "green" quality gem (e.g., Bloodstone, Sun Crystal).

The Strategy: Check which gem is required for the daily quest in Dalaran. If the quest is "Shipment: Bloodstone Band," the price of Bloodstones will triple for that day only. Sell your stockpile of that specific color immediately.

5. Phase Transition Timing (The "Ulduar" Prep)

Since Onyxia is in Phase 1 (Naxxramas), you need to look ahead to Phase 2 (Ulduar).

When to Sell Titansteel: Sell your Titansteel weapons (Destroyers) now. Once Phase 2/Ulduar launches, these items will be replaced by better raid drops, and their value will crash.

When to Hold: Start hoarding Titanium Ore and Eternal Fires about 3–4 weeks before Phase 2 is announced. When Ulduar drops, new powerful Engineering goggles and JC recipes will be released, and the price of these mats will skyrocket.
